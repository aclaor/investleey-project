Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Investleey ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our service at investleey.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account (email address, password)
  • Set up your business profile (business name, description, industry)
  • Connect social media accounts (access tokens, page IDs)
  • Create and schedule posts (post content, images)
  • Make a payment (processed securely by PayPal)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Publish posts to your connected social media accounts on your behalf
  • Send you service-related notifications
  • Process payments and prevent fraud
  • Generate AI-written content based on your business profile

3. Social Media Connections

When you connect your social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, Discord, Telegram, etc.), we store access tokens securely to provide stock analysis and forecasts. We only post content that you have explicitly scheduled or approved. We do not read your private messages or access data beyond what is necessary to publish posts.

4. Facebook Data

When you connect your Facebook Page, we access:

  • Your Facebook Page information (name, ID)
  • The ability to publish posts to your Page on your behalf

We do not sell your Facebook data to third parties. You can disconnect your Facebook account at any time from your dashboard. To request deletion of your Facebook data, email us at alexclaor@gmail.com.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database provider. Access tokens for social media platforms are encrypted at rest. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Social media platforms (to publish your posts)
  • Payment processors (PayPal, for billing)
  • AI providers (Cloudflare Workers AI, to generate post content)
  • Infrastructure providers (Railway, Supabase, Cloudflare)

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect any social media account at any time
  • Export your post history

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on our website.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request deletion of your data, please contact us at:

Email: alexclaor@gmail.com
